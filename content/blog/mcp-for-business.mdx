date: 2025-02-20

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) standardises how assistants access context and tools. For teams, this means:

1. A secure way to expose data and actions
2. Consistent governance and observability
3. Composability across assistants and vendors

We’re helping clients build providers for knowledge, search, analytics and operational actions — safely and incrementally.
